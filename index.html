    1. Visa Assistance

    Applying for or maintaining U.S. permanent residency can be challenging. Immigration Questions provides guidance on family-based green cards, employment-based green cards, and other paths to permanent residency, ensuring your application is complete and timely.

    3. Citizenship and Naturalization Guidance

    If you're ready to take the next step toward U.S. citizenship, our experts can walk you through the naturalization process. We provide detailed information on eligibility requirements, application procedures, and interview preparation.

    4. Family Immigration

    Reuniting families is one of the most rewarding aspects of U.S. immigration law. We offer advice on family-based petitions, such as sponsoring spouses, children, parents, and siblings for green cards, helping families navigate the immigration process smoothly.

    5. Asylum and Refugee Applications

    For individuals seeking protection in the U.S., we provide expert legal support for asylum and refugee applications. Our team can assist in preparing your case, gathering necessary documentation, and representing your interests before immigration authorities.

    6. Deportation Defense

    Facing deportation or removal proceedings is a serious matter. Immigration Questions provides strategic legal advice and representation to defend your rights and help you stay in the U.S.

    7. DACA and TPS

    For individuals covered under Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals (DACA) or Temporary Protected Status (TPS), we offer guidance on renewals, eligibility, and how to maintain your immigration status.
